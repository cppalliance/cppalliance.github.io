Fernando, a second-generation programmer, was inspired by his father's profession and embarked on his programming journey from an early age. His professional career in programming was launched in 1997, and since then he has gained extensive experience in C++.

His noteworthy contributions to the field have earned him the “Senator Domingo Faustino Sarmiento” Honorable Mention, a highly esteemed recognition by the Argentine Parliament.

In recent years, Fernando has been making significant contributions to the world of Bitcoin and Blockchain development. He maintains and contributes to several key Bitcoin and Bitcoin Cash node implementations, including Knuth, Bitcoin Cash Node (BCHN), and Bitcoin-ABC. Notably, Knuth is a prominent full-node implementation supporting Bitcoin (BTC), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), and Litecoin (LTC).
