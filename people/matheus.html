Matheus Izvekov is a compiler engineer, long time contributor to Clang and a C++ committee member. He maintains the Clang C++ template engine and AST. He has many contributions on improving template argument deduction, partial ordering, NRVO, among others. He maintains the clang template specialization resugaring fork, which he hopes to upstream. Outside of computer science, he likes to hike, play board games, D&D and spending time on the beach.
